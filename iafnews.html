Indian Air Force News

  • IAF Pilots Conduct Successful Exercise with US Navy

    Indian Air Force pilots recently conducted a successful exercise with the US Navy in the Pacific Ocean. The exercise involved practicing aerial refueling, close air support, and air defense tactics.

  • IAF Receives New Batch of Rafale Fighter Jets

    The Indian Air Force recently received a new batch of Rafale fighter jets from France. The Rafale is a multirole fighter jet that is capable of carrying out a variety of missions, including air-to-air combat, air-to-ground strikes, and reconnaissance.
