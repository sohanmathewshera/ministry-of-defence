About the Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. It is one of the most powerful air forces in the world, with over 500 aircraft and over 170,000 personnel.

The IAF's primary mission is to defend India's airspace and maritime borders. It also plays a key role in supporting the Indian Army and Navy in their operations.

The IAF has a long and proud history of service to the nation. It has played a vital role in defending India against aggression, and has also participated in peacekeeping missions around the world.

Careers with the IAF

The IAF offers a wide range of career opportunities for men and women. There are opportunities for pilots, navigators, technicians, and support personnel.

If you are interested in a challenging and rewarding career, the IAF is the place for you.
