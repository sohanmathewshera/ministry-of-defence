Indian Air Force Careers

Flying Branch

The flying branch of the Indian Air Force is responsible for operating and flying various types of aircraft, including fighter jets, transport aircraft, and helicopters.

  • Pilot
  • Navigator
  • Flight Engineer

Non-Flying Branch

The non-flying branch of the Indian Air Force offers a variety of career opportunities in fields such as administration, engineering, logistics, and medicine.

  • Ground Duty Officer
  • Technical Officer
  • Aeronautical Engineer (Mechanical)

How to Join

To join the Indian Air Force, you must meet the following eligibility criteria:

  • Age: Between 16.5 and 19 years
  • Educational qualification: Class 12th with a minimum of 50% marks in Mathematics and Physics
  • Physical fitness: Meet the IAF's physical fitness standards

Benefits

The Indian Air Force offers a number of benefits to its personnel, including:

  • Competitive pay and allowances
  • Medical benefits
  • Housing benefits
  • Educational benefits
  • Retirement benefits
